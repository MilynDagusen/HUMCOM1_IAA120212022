MY FAVORITES

Born To Beat (BTOB)

A k-pop boy band created by Vube Entertainment, BTOB debuted in March 2012 and rose to mainstream success over the next years with a chart-topping releases like 2014's Beep Beep EP and the 2015 album Complete. BTOB debuted as a seven member group consisting Seo Eunkwang, Lee Minhyuk, Lee Changsub, Im Hyunsik, Peniel, Yook Sungjae and Jung Ilhoon.

Sen Çal Kapimi

Eda, who pins all her hopes on her education, confronts Serkan Bolat, who has cut off her international scholarship, which leaves her with a high school diploma only. Serkan offers Eda to give her scholarship back on condition that she pretends to be his fiancée for two months. Although Eda initially rejects the offer out of hand, she sees herself compelled to accept it when conditions change overnight. While pretending to be engaged, Serkan and Eda embark on an insanely passionate yet challenging relationship that will make them forget all they know that is right.

911

The series explores the high-pressure experiences of first responders -- including police officers, firefighters and dispatchers -- who are thrust into the most frightening, shocking and heart-stopping situations. These emergency responders must try to balance saving those who are at their most vulnerable with solving the problems in their own lives. The show draws from real-life, high-pressure experiences of first responders who regularly face heart-stopping situations that are often unpredictable, intense and uplifting at the same time.
