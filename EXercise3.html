HOW TO STREAM A MUSIC VIDEO PROPERLY

How to Stream

STREAMING USING PREMIUM YOUTUBE ACCOUNT

  1. Sign-in. Search for the music video MANUALLY. Watch it from the OFFICIAL CHANNEL
  2. Like the video (after 30 seconds or 1 minute). Share the link of the music video to any social media platforms.
  3. Follow these playback to make sure your view counts.
    • Quality should be at 480p and above
    • Volume MUST be atleast 50%
    • Normal speed
    • Watch the FULL MV (DO NOT SKIP)
  4. Watch 3-4 other videos (MUST BE 3-4 minutes long) before streaming again.
TIP: YOUTUBE PREMIUM as it weighs more on chart and NO ADS
