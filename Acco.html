BAGUIO ACCOMODATIONS

Baguio City is called the summer capital for a reason: its cool, breezy weather is a perfect breather from the hot and humid city any time of the year! If you’re planning to visit the City of Pines and its numerous must-see attractions, but are on a tight budget, no problem. You can book your stay at these Baguio hotels for only P2,000 and below. Whether you’re planning to stay for 1 night or 3, these cheap hotels in Baguio will ensure you have a comfortable vacation!

1. Holiday Inn

The holidays are always so much better with family, so if you’re travelling to Baguio with the entire clan, Holiday Inn in Baguio City Centre is the place for you! It’s a family-friendly accommodation, not only offering amenities suitable for kids, but each room is spacious enough for everyone to enjoy. You can also take the fun outside to the pool or recreational area, or walk a short distance to the city’s top attractions.

2. The Manor

The Manor by Camp John Hay still reigns as the most sought-after accommodation in the city. With the wooden cabin-style lodging and the sea of pine trees covering the property, there’s something truly magical, and for many, nostalgic, about a staycation at The Manor. During the holidays, the accommodation becomes a place of congregation for family and friends to watch the Christmas lights and converse by the fire. More than just an accommodation, you’re guaranteed beautiful memories at The Manor.

3. Forest Lodge

Similar to The Manor, The Forest Lodge doesn’t fall short in terms of comfort and providing a true Baguio experience. The only difference between the two accommodations is the location. If you’re looking for something more quiet and low-key, the Forest Lodge is the right pick.

4. Grand Sierra Pines Hotel

For those more interested in a relaxing staycation, the Grand Sierra Pines hotel is a great option. It’s nestled in a less commercial side of Baguio, offering guests a quiet environment for a truly restful experience. You can choose from a Superior Room, Deluxe Room, or Family Room depending on your preference; but each room is designed thoughtfully to provide both comfort and luxury for its guests.

5. Kamiseta Hotel

You wanted an Instagrammable accommodation? Well, look no further. The Kamiseta Hotel is as Instagrammable as it gets - and we’re totally here for it! Since we haven’t had the chance to travel and fill up our Instagram feeds in the nine months we’ve been in lockdown, you’ll definitely get a good number of photo opportunities in this hotel alone.

6. Orchard Hotel

In Baguio, travelers are more likely to come across decades-old cabins, so the Orchard Hotel’s minimalist and modern design is a really refreshing choice especially for returning travelers. It has also claimed a millennial approach to their design, making sure every angle of the hotel is Instagram-friendly.

7. Tudor in the Pines

Tudor in the Pines offers a unique and safe staycation option for romantic getaways, intimate group reunions, and family road trips. Nestled among the dense foliage of Baguio City’s pinewood forest, this Airbnb property offers seven unique residences within a gated property, and can accommodate a maximum of 30 guests.With the unique accommodations, the convenience of multiple road accesses to and from the city, and assured safety standards, Tudor in the Pines offers an ideal home base for those with their own vehicles who want to enjoy the Northern wonders of Luzon.
